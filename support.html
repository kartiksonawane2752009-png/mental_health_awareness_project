Helplines

Indian mental health helplines

All helplines below are confidential. Most are free of cost. You can call even if you're not in a crisis — they're there to listen.

🆘

iCall

TISS Psychosocial Helpline

Free counselling by trained psychologists for emotional and mental health concerns. Great for students.

9152987821 Mon–Sat, 8am–10pm
🌿

Kiran Helpline

Government of India

National 24/7 mental health helpline. Toll-free and available in 13 Indian languages across all states.

1800-599-0019 24 hours, 7 days — Toll-Free
💙

Vandrevala Foundation

Crisis Support

24/7 helpline for mental health crises, depression, anxiety, and suicidal thoughts. Judgment-free.

1860-2662-345 24 hours, 7 days
🏥

NIMHANS

National Institute

National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences. Expert psychiatric support and guidance.

080-46110007 Mon–Sat, 9am–5pm
🤲

SNEHI

Emotional Support

Non-profit offering compassionate support for distress, loneliness, and suicidal ideation. Volunteer-run.

044-24640050 Daily, 8am–10pm
🕊️

Sumaitri

Delhi Crisis Line

Confidential, non-judgmental emotional support for people in distress and suicidal ideation.

011-23389090 Daily, 2pm–10pm
